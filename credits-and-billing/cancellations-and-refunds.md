# Cancellations and Refunds

Use the billing area in Hollapic or the payment-management option connected to your subscription to manage cancellation.

## When you cancel

When a subscription is scheduled to cancel at the end of the billing period, access and subscription credits normally remain available until that paid period ends.

After the subscription fully ends, subscription credits are removed. Purchased or bonus credits remain subject to their own rules.

## Refunds

Review the current **Refund and Cancellation Policy** shown in Hollapic before purchasing.

Because payment and refund eligibility can depend on the purchase and current policy, contact support when you believe a payment was made in error.

## Before contacting support

Prepare:

- The email used for the purchase
- The approximate payment date and time
- The plan or credit purchase
- A screenshot of the payment confirmation
- A short explanation of the issue

## Billing did not update

If payment succeeded but your plan or credits did not update:

1. Refresh Hollapic.
2. Confirm you are signed in with the purchasing email.
3. Wait a few moments and check again.
4. Contact support with the payment details.
