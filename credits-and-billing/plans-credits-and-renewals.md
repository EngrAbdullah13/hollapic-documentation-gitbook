# Plans, Credits, and Renewals

Hollapic uses credits for image generation. The plans and credit amounts available to you appear on the Hollapic pricing page.

## How credits work

Your available balance may include:

* **Subscription credits** from an active paid plan
* **Purchased credits** bought as a one-time credit purchase
* **Bonus credits** added through signup or an approved promotion

The credit balance shown in Studio reflects your current account or selected workspace.

## Buy a plan

1. Open the Hollapic pricing page.
2. Choose the plan that matches your expected image usage.
3. Complete checkout.
4. Return to Hollapic and refresh your credit balance if needed.

## Buy custom credits

Custom credits are one-time purchases.

You can buy them only after you have successfully purchased an eligible standard plan at least once or currently have a qualifying paid plan.

1. Open Studio.
2. Select the **plus** action near your credits.
3. Choose the credit amount.
4. Complete checkout.

## Subscription renewals

When a subscription renews successfully, its subscription-credit balance resets to the monthly amount for that plan. Unused subscription credits do not stack on top of the new monthly amount.

Purchased and bonus credits are separate from subscription credits.

## Credit expiration

Subscription credits are available for their billing period. Purchased and bonus credits follow the rules shown in your account and plan information.

## Shared workspaces

Only the workspace owner can manage billing or buy credits for that workspace.
