# Product Infographic

Product Infographic creates ecommerce feature callout images.

## Before you begin

- Upload a clear product image.
- Prepare the feature points you want shown as callouts.

## Expected result

The product should remain clear and central, with space for clean callouts, icons, arrows, and benefits.

## Important behavior

Visible text should be supplied by the user when exact text is required. Do not rely on the model to infer legally or commercially important wording.
