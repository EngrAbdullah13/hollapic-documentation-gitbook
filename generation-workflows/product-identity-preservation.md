# Product identity preservation

Product identity preservation is a core prompt-planning rule across workflows.

## Preserved details

Hollapic prompt planning instructs the model to preserve:

- Product silhouette and construction.
- Product proportions and packaging geometry.
- Product colors, materials, patterns, and textures.
- Logos and visible branding.
- Label placement and all legible product text.
- Cap, lid, bottle, jar, box, pouch, or package structure.

## Important behavior

Product identity has higher priority than Brand DNA, reference-image style, and general aesthetic guidance.
