# Human model behavior

Some workflows can use people, hands, or human lifestyle scenes when the source or reference suggests that direction.

## Important behavior

- People connected to the product presentation may be preserved in Background Replace.
- In Style Copy, if a person appears in the reference, the planner asks for a different person in a similar role.
- The system should not recreate the same person from a reference image.

## Best practices

- Clearly say whether you want a hand, model, or no human.
- Describe the role, crop, pose, or interaction rather than a specific person.
