# Product Swap

Product Swap places your uploaded product into a reference scene.

## Before you begin

- Image 1 must be the product source.
- Image 2 must be the reference scene.

## Steps

1. Select **Product Swap**.
2. Upload your product image.
3. Upload the reference scene.
4. Add optional instructions if needed.
5. Generate the image.

## Expected result

The generated image should preserve the reference scene environment, lighting, shadows, perspective, camera angle, and realism while replacing only the main product/object with your uploaded product.

## Important behavior

Product Swap should not replace unrelated background objects or unnecessarily change the scene.
