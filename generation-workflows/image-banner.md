# Image Banner

Image Banner creates professional ecommerce marketing or advertisement banner images.

## Before you begin

- Upload the product image.
- Choose a wide aspect ratio such as 16:9 when the creative is intended for banner placement.

## Steps

1. Select **Image Banner**.
2. Upload the product image.
3. Add optional copy or campaign direction only if you want visible text.
4. Choose aspect ratio and quality.
5. Generate the banner.

## Expected result

The product remains the main focus while the output uses a professional marketing composition.
