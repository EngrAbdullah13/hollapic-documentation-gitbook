# General Image Generation

General Image creates a complete commercial product image, lifestyle image, or advertising scene around the uploaded product.

## Before you begin

- Upload a product image.
- Add optional answers or custom instructions if you have a specific direction.

## Steps

1. Select **General Image**.
2. Upload the product image.
3. Choose aspect ratio and quality.
4. Add optional answers, custom instructions, or Brand DNA.
5. Generate the image.

## Expected result

The result should feel like a complete ecommerce product photo or lifestyle/ad scene, not simply a cutout pasted into a background.

## Important behavior

If no answers are provided, the prompt planner creates a strong default ecommerce or lifestyle scene from the product image, workflow, aspect ratio, and Brand DNA if enabled.
