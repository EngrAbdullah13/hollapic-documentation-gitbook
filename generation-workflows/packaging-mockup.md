# Packaging Mockup

Packaging Mockup creates realistic presentations of packaging such as boxes, bottles, tubes, jars, or pouches.

## Before you begin

- Upload a clear source image of the product or packaging identity.
- Add optional instructions for material, surface, or setting.

## Expected result

The output should preserve visible brand and product identity while presenting packaging in a realistic commercial mockup.
