# Shared generation history

Generations created by collaborators are saved under the owner workspace.

## Stored attribution

Generation jobs store:

- `workspaceOwnerId`
- `generatedByUserId`
- `workspaceMemberId`
- `creditsUsed`
- `accessTypeAtGeneration`

## Owner experience

Owners can see images generated in their workspace and attribution for who generated them.
