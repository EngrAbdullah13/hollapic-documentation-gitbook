# Full credit access

Full credit access is shared credit access. It is not unlimited access.

## Behavior

Full-credit collaborators use the owner’s current free/shared credit pool. They cannot spend credits reserved for limited-credit collaborators.

## Example

| Owner total | Reserved limited | Free/shared |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 500 | 300 | 200 |

A full-credit collaborator can spend from the 200 free/shared credits, subject to owner balance and backend checks.
