# Workspace troubleshooting

Use this page for shared workspace issues.

## Invitation link expired

Ask the owner to send a new invitation. Expired pending invitations no longer grant access.

## Wrong email address

Accept invitations with the email address that received the invite.

## Credits look lower than owner total

Collaborators see the credits available for their access type, not necessarily the owner’s full credit balance.
