# Invite and Manage Collaborators

Shared Workspaces let an owner invite an agency or teammate without sharing the account password.

The collaborator works inside the owner’s workspace and uses the access granted by the owner.

## Invite a collaborator

1. Make sure you are in your personal workspace.
2. Open the user menu.
3. Select **Invite Team**.
4. Select **Invite Collaborator**.
5. Enter the collaborator’s email address.
6. Enter the workspace name they should see.
7. Choose **Full Credit Access** or **Limited Credit Access**.
8. For limited access, enter the credit allowance.
9. Send the invitation.

## What happens next

The collaborator receives an email with an invitation link. After they accept, the shared workspace appears in their workspace switcher.

## Manage collaborators

From **Invite Team**, an owner can:

- Review pending and active invitations
- Change access type or limited credit allowance
- Check collaborator usage
- Remove access

## What collaborators can do

A collaborator can create and download images in the shared workspace while access is active.

They cannot manage the owner’s billing or invite other people unless the product later adds that permission.
