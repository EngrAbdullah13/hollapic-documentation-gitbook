# Switch Workspaces and Manage Credits

The workspace switcher lets you move between your personal workspace and workspaces shared with you.

## Switch workspaces

1. Open the workspace switcher in the Studio header.
2. Choose your personal workspace or a shared workspace.
3. Wait for the workspace name and available credits to update.
4. Create images in the selected workspace.

## Full Credit Access

A collaborator with full access can use the owner workspace’s currently available shared credits.

Credits reserved for other limited collaborators are not included.

## Limited Credit Access

A collaborator with limited access can use only the allowance assigned by the owner.

The collaborator sees the remaining amount from that allowance, not the owner’s full credit total.

## Generation history

Images created in a shared workspace are saved in the owner’s workspace history.

## Before generating

Always check:

- The correct workspace is selected.
- The displayed credits match the workspace you intend to use.
- Your access is still active.
