# Invite an agency or collaborator

Owners can invite collaborators by email from the Invite Team page.

{% hint style="info" %}
Screenshot required: Invite Collaborator modal showing agency email, workspace name, access type, and limited credit amount.
{% endhint %}

## Before you begin

- You must be working in your own workspace.
- You need the collaborator email address.
- For limited credit access, you need enough unreserved free/shared credits.

## Steps

1. Open the user menu.
2. Select **Invite Team**.
3. Select **Invite Collaborator**.
4. Enter the agency email.
5. Enter the workspace name shown to the agency.
6. Choose full credit access or limited credit access.
7. For limited access, set the credit limit.
8. Send the invitation.

## Expected result

The collaborator receives an invitation email with an acceptance link. Pending limited invitations reserve the assigned credits immediately.
