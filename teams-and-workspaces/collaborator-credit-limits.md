# Set collaborator credit limits

Owners can assign or edit limited credit allocations.

## Rules

- Limited allocation is reserved immediately for pending invitations and active limited members.
- The owner cannot assign more than current free/shared credits.
- The owner can increase an allocation only when enough free/shared credits exist.
- The owner can decrease an allocation only to at least the credits already used by that collaborator.

## Owner view

The Invite Team page shows total credits, reserved team credits, free/shared credits, collaborator access type, assigned credits, used credits, and remaining credits.
