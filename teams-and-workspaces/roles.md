# Owner and collaborator roles

Shared Workspace has two phase-one roles.

## Role table

| Role | Can do | Cannot do |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Owner | Manage credits, billing, Brand DNA, generation history, invites, access, and workspace data. | Not applicable. |
| Collaborator | Generate and download images in shared workspaces, view shared history allowed by workspace access. | Manage billing, buy credits, invite others, delete owner account, or transfer credits. |
