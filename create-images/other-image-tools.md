# Other Image Tools

Hollapic may show additional tools for specific ecommerce image tasks.

## Label Generation

Use this to create a label or packaging concept.

Upload your logo or brand source first. Add a product or container image when the label needs to fit a specific surface. Provide the exact words you want visible.

## Product to Full Image

This creates a complete product or lifestyle scene. In the current product, it follows the same general purpose as General Image Generation.

## Image Banner

Use this for wide ecommerce or advertising images. Choose **16:9** when you need a horizontal banner.

## Packaging Mockup

Use this to present boxes, bottles, tubes, jars, or pouches in a realistic commercial setting.

## Collection Image

Use this to show several products together. Upload the product images in the order you want Hollapic to understand them. When a reference image is used, add it after the product images.

## Product Infographic

Use this to create a product-focused layout with feature callouts, icons, arrows, or benefits.

Provide the exact text when wording must be correct. Always review generated text before publishing.
