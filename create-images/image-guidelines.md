# Image Guidelines

A clear source image makes it easier for Hollapic to preserve your product.

## Use a source image where

- The full product is visible.
- The label, logo, colors, and packaging shape are easy to see.
- The image is sharp and well lit.
- Important edges are not cropped.
- Other objects do not cover the product.

## Avoid images where

- The label is too small or blurry to read.
- The product is hidden behind props or hands.
- Several similar products make the main product unclear.
- Strong shadows hide the packaging shape.
- The image has already been heavily edited or compressed.

## Recommended formats

Use:

- **PNG** for transparent backgrounds and clean product cutouts
- **JPG or JPEG** for normal product photography

When another format is rejected, convert it to PNG or JPG and upload it again.

## Choose the right size

| Size | Best for |
| --- | --- |
| **1:1** | Square product images and social posts |
| **4:5** | Portrait and mobile-first content |
| **16:9** | Website banners and wide ads |
| **4:3** | Balanced product and lifestyle images |

## Write clear instructions

A useful instruction usually includes:

1. The type of scene
2. The surface or environment
3. The lighting
4. The camera view
5. Important product details that must stay unchanged

## Review before publishing

Check:

- Product shape
- Label and logo
- Product color
- Visible text
- Product scale
- Shadows and reflections
- Hands, faces, and small details
