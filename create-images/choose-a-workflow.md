# Choose the Right Workflow

Choose a workflow based on the change you want to make.

| You want to… | Choose |
| --- | --- |
| Create a complete commercial or lifestyle image | **General Image Generation** |
| Keep the product and change its surroundings | **Background Replace** |
| Place your product into another image’s scene | **Product Swap** |
| Use another image for mood, lighting, and photography style | **Style Copy** |
| Create a label or packaging concept | **Label Generation** |
| Create a banner, collection image, mockup, or infographic | **Other Image Tools** |

## Quick decision guide

Choose **General Image Generation** when you have a product and want Hollapic to build the full scene.

Choose **Background Replace** when the current product position is already good and only the background should change.

Choose **Product Swap** when you have a target scene and want your product placed into it.

Choose **Style Copy** when you like the mood or visual style of another image but do not want to copy the exact scene.

## Important

The order of uploaded images matters for Product Swap and Style Copy. Always upload your product first and the reference image second.
