# Product Infographic

Use Product Infographic to create a product-focused image with feature, benefit, ingredient, dimension, or usage callouts.

## How to generate an image

1. Sign up or log in to Hollapic and open **Studio**.
2. Set up Brand DNA if you want Hollapic to follow your saved brand direction. This is optional.
3. Select **Product Infographic**.
4. Upload the required product image and any supporting assets shown in Studio.
5. Add the exact text, benefits, features, icons, arrows, or layout details you want to include.
6. Choose the image size and resolution or quality.
7. Turn on Brand DNA if you have already set it up and want to use it for this generation.
8. Select **Generate**.

## Example instruction

> Create a clean product infographic with three benefit callouts and simple icons. Keep the product label unchanged.

## What happens next

Hollapic creates a product infographic based on your image and instructions.

Review the product, label, logo, colors, text, and overall image before downloading or copying it.
