# Generation Type Use Cases

## Background Replace

Use it to create:

* Apply the background style of a reference image to your product &#x20;
* Replace a plain background with a studio, lifestyle, outdoor, or seasonal scene&#x20;
* Match the colors, lighting, and mood of a selected reference background

## Product Swap

Use it to:

* Place your product into a reference advertisement
* Replace another product in a lifestyle scene
* Reuse a strong composition with your own product
* Match the lighting and camera angle of a target scene

## Style Copy

Use it to follow:

* Lighting style
* Mood and color grading
* Photography style
* Camera distance and composition
* A similar human pose or role with a different person

## Label Generation

Use it to create:

* Product-label concepts
* Packaging-label ideas
* New label directions using your logo and colors
* Label presentations on bottles, jars, boxes, or pouches

## Image Banner

Use it to create:

* Website hero banners
* Collection banners
* Promotional campaign banners
* Wide advertising images
* Images with space for a headline or button

## Packaging Mockup

Use it to create:

* Box mockups
* Bottle and jar presentations
* Tube or pouch mockups
* Premium studio packaging scenes
* Packaging concepts for client presentations

## Collection Image

Use it to create:

* Product-family images
* Bundles and sets
* Collection headers
* Several products arranged in one scene
* Routine or range visuals

## Product Infographic

Use it to create:

* Product-benefit images
* Feature callouts
* Ingredient highlights
* Size or dimension explanations
* Simple usage steps

Always review generated text before publishing an infographic.

## General Image Generation

Upload your product, choose a style, add optional details, select size and quality, then click **Generate**.

* **Professional Studio:** Clean images for product pages and ads.
* **Product With Human:** Show someone holding or using the product.
* **Luxury Lifestyle:** Create premium, elegant scenes.
* **Minimal Background:** Keep the focus on the product.
* **Outdoor Nature:** Place the product in a natural setting.

For better results, choose a ready-made option, write a custom prompt, or answer the optional questions.
