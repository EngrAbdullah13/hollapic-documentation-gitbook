# Generation Type Use Cases

This page gives you simple examples of what you can create with each Hollapic generation type.

## General Image Generation

Use it to create:

- Ecommerce lifestyle images
- Product campaign visuals
- Social media product images
- Commercial product photography
- Seasonal or themed product scenes

## Background Replace

Use it to create:

- Clean studio backgrounds
- Luxury bathroom or vanity scenes
- Outdoor or natural environments
- Seasonal backgrounds
- Brand-colored environments

## Product Swap

Use it to:

- Place your product into a reference advertisement
- Replace another product in a lifestyle scene
- Reuse a strong composition with your own product
- Match the lighting and camera angle of a target scene

## Style Copy

Use it to follow:

- Lighting style
- Mood and color grading
- Photography style
- Camera distance and composition
- A similar human pose or role with a different person

## Label Generation

Use it to create:

- Product-label concepts
- Packaging-label ideas
- New label directions using your logo and colors
- Label presentations on bottles, jars, boxes, or pouches

## Image Banner

Use it to create:

- Website hero banners
- Collection banners
- Promotional campaign banners
- Wide advertising images
- Images with space for a headline or button

## Packaging Mockup

Use it to create:

- Box mockups
- Bottle and jar presentations
- Tube or pouch mockups
- Premium studio packaging scenes
- Packaging concepts for client presentations

## Collection Image

Use it to create:

- Product-family images
- Bundles and sets
- Collection headers
- Several products arranged in one scene
- Routine or range visuals

## Product Infographic

Use it to create:

- Product-benefit images
- Feature callouts
- Ingredient highlights
- Size or dimension explanations
- Simple usage steps

Always review generated text before publishing an infographic.

## Product to Full Image

Use it to create:

- A full lifestyle image from a simple product photo
- A complete commercial scene
- A social or ecommerce product visual
- A polished image with environment, lighting, and props
