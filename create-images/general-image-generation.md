# General Image Generation

General Image Generation creates a complete commercial or lifestyle image around your uploaded product.

Use it for product photography, campaign visuals, website images, social content, and ecommerce lifestyle scenes.

## Before you begin

Upload a clear product image. A reference image is optional unless the Studio asks for one.

## How to do it

1. Select **General Image Generation**.
2. Upload your product image.
3. Describe the scene you want.
4. Choose an image size.
5. Turn on Brand DNA when you want the result to follow your saved brand direction.
6. Select **Generate**.

## Example instruction

> Create a premium bathroom lifestyle image with warm natural light, soft shadows, and neutral stone details. Keep the product bottle, label, logo, colors, and shape unchanged.

## What happens next

Hollapic creates a finished scene with your product as the main focus.

## Tips for better results

- Mention the setting and surface.
- Describe the lighting and mood.
- Tell Hollapic where the product should appear.
- Add only the props that support the product.
- State clearly which product details must stay unchanged.
