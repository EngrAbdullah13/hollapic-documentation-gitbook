# Generate your first image

This guide walks through the simplest first generation.

## Before you begin

- You must be signed in.
- Your active workspace must have enough credits.
- Use a product image where the product is visible and not heavily cropped.

## Steps

1. In Studio, choose **General Image** if you want Hollapic to create a full commercial product scene.
2. Upload your product image.
3. Keep the default aspect ratio if you do not have a specific placement requirement.
4. Leave optional answers blank if you want Hollapic to infer a professional ecommerce scene.
5. Select **Generate**.
6. Wait for the job to complete.
7. Use the output node download button to save the result.

## Expected result

The output should be a polished commercial image that uses the uploaded product as the product identity source.

## Important behavior

The prompt planner treats the uploaded product as source identity. It should not intentionally redesign packaging, labels, logos, product shape, or visible product text.

## Troubleshooting

If the product changes too much, see [Product appearance changed](../troubleshooting/product-appearance-changed.md).
