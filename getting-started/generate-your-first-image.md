# Generate Your First Image

This guide takes you through the complete Hollapic process, from signing in to downloading your generated image.

{% hint style="info" %}
Video: **Hollapic Quick Start** — show how to sign in, choose a generation type, upload the required images, select the image size and resolution, generate the image, and download the result.
{% endhint %}

## 1. Sign up or log in

Create your Hollapic account, or log in if you already have one. After signing in, open **Studio**.

[Account and Login](https://app.gitbook.com/s/CJGLpzM7Cg0SmAIpze7V/account-and-login "mention")

## 2. Set up Brand DNA if you want

Brand DNA is optional. It helps Hollapic understand your brand style, audience, preferred colors, and visual direction.

You can set it up before your first generation or skip it and continue directly to Studio.

[Brand DNA](https://app.gitbook.com/s/CJGLpzM7Cg0SmAIpze7V/brand-dna "mention")

## 3. Select a generation type

Choose the generation type that matches the image you want to create. For example:

* Use **General Image Generation** for a complete commercial or lifestyle image.
* Use **Background Replace** to change the environment around your product.
* Use **Product Swap** to place your product into a reference scene.
* Use **Style Copy** to follow the lighting, mood, and photography style of another image.

[Create Images](https://app.gitbook.com/s/CJGLpzM7Cg0SmAIpze7V/create-images "mention")

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-07-16 163511.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## 4. Upload the required images

Each generation type shows which images are required.

Upload the required product or reference images in the correct upload areas. When Hollapic shows an optional upload area, you can leave it empty or add an extra reference image when it will help explain the result you want.

## 5. Add optional details

You can add custom instructions, extra guidance, or other optional details. Keep your instructions clear and focused on the result you want.

Example:

> Create a premium skincare lifestyle image on a warm stone surface with soft morning light. Keep the bottle, label, logo, colors, and packaging shape unchanged.

## 6. Turn on Brand DNA if it is ready

When you have already completed Brand DNA, turn on the Brand DNA option to guide the image toward your saved brand direction.

This step is optional.

## 7. Select the image size and resolution

Choose the aspect ratio or image size that fits where you plan to use the image. Then select the resolution or quality you need.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-07-16 172749.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## 8. Generate the image

Select **Generate**. Hollapic will create your image and show the result in Studio.

Review the product, label, logo, colors, text, and overall scene. When you are happy with the result, download or copy the image.
