# Create Your First Image

Use this guide when you are ready to create your first Hollapic image.

## Before you begin

Prepare a product image that is:

- Sharp and well lit
- Large enough to show the label clearly
- Not cropped around important product edges
- Free from objects that hide the product

PNG is useful for transparent product images. JPG or JPEG works well for normal product photography.

## How to do it

1. Open **Hollapic Studio**.
2. Select **General Image Generation** for a complete commercial or lifestyle image.
3. Upload your product image.
4. Write a short description of the image you want.
5. Choose an image size:
   - **1:1** for square store or social images
   - **4:5** for portrait and mobile-first images
   - **16:9** for wide banners
   - **4:3** for balanced product images
6. Select **Generate**.
7. Review the product, label, logo, colors, and overall scene.
8. Download the image or adjust your instructions and try again.

## Tips for your first result

- Describe the setting, lighting, mood, and camera view.
- Tell Hollapic what must stay unchanged.
- Do not ask for several very different scenes in one generation.
- Use a clear source image with readable branding.
