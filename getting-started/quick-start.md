This guide shows the basic Hollapic workflow from upload to download.

## Create an image

1. Sign in to Hollapic and open **Studio**.
2. Choose the workflow that matches the result you need.
3. Upload a clear image of your product.
4. Add a reference image when the selected workflow needs one.
5. Describe the scene, mood, lighting, or result you want.
6. Choose an image size.
7. Select **Generate**.
8. Review the result and download it when you are happy.

## A simple instruction example

> Create a clean skincare lifestyle image on a warm stone surface with soft morning light. Keep the bottle, label, logo, colors, and packaging shape unchanged.

Clear instructions work better than long or conflicting instructions.

## What happens next

Hollapic creates the image and shows it in Studio. The generation also appears in your history, where you can review or download it later.
