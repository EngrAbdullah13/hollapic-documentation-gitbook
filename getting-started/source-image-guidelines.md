# Recommended source image guidelines

Good source images improve product preservation and generation quality.

## Use images where

- The product is clearly visible.
- Packaging shape, label, logo, and colors are readable.
- The image is not heavily blurred.
- Important edges are not cut off.
- The product is not hidden behind unrelated objects.

## Avoid images where

- The product label is too small to read.
- The product is at an extreme angle unless that angle is intentional.
- The lighting hides the package shape.
- The product is mixed with many similar products when the workflow expects one main product.
