# What is Hollapic?

Hollapic helps ecommerce brands create product images for online stores, ads, social media, and marketing campaigns.

You start with a real product image. You then choose what you want Hollapic to create, such as a lifestyle photo, a new background, a product placed into a reference scene, or a design inspired by another image.

## What you can create

You can use Hollapic to create:

- Commercial product and lifestyle images
- New product backgrounds
- Product placements based on a reference scene
- Images inspired by a reference style
- Banners, collection images, packaging concepts, and infographics
- Brand-consistent visuals using Brand DNA

## How Hollapic protects your product

For the best result, always upload a clear image where the product shape, label, logo, and colors are easy to see. Hollapic uses your uploaded product as the main source for the new image.

AI-generated images can still vary. Review each result before using it in your store or advertising.

## What you need

- A Hollapic account
- Available image credits
- A clear product image
- A simple description of the result you want
