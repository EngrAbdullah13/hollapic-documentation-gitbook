## Create product images

Hollapic includes different generation types for different jobs. You can create a complete lifestyle image, replace a background, place your product into another scene, copy a visual style, create banners, mockups, infographics, and more.

[Explore generation types](create-images/select-generation-type.md)

## Keep your brand consistent

Set up Brand DNA with your brand style, audience, preferred colors, and website. When Brand DNA is ready, you can turn it on while generating images.

[Set Up Brand DNA](brand-dna/set-up-brand-dna.md)
