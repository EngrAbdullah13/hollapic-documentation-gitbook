# Review generated results

After a job completes, review the output for product accuracy, visual quality, and channel suitability.

## Check these areas

- Product shape and proportions.
- Logo, label, and readable product text.
- Product colors and packaging material.
- Lighting, shadows, and realism.
- Whether the output matches the chosen workflow.
- Whether humans or hands are appropriate and not copied from references.
