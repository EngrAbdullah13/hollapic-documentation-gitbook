# Billing problems

Use this page when checkout, plan purchases, or custom credits do not work.

## Common causes

- You are in a collaborator workspace.
- Custom credit eligibility has not been met.
- Stripe checkout was abandoned or failed.
- Webhook processing has not completed yet.
