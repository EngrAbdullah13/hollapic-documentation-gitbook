# Product appearance changed

Use this guide when product colors, materials, label placement, or recognizable details changed too much.

## Fixes

1. Use a clearer product image.
2. Add instructions such as “preserve the exact product, label, logo, colors, and packaging shape.”
3. Choose a workflow that matches the task.
4. Avoid reference images with competing products that could confuse the result.
