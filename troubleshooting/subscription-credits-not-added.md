# Subscription credits were not added

Subscription credits are set from successful Stripe checkout or renewal events.

## Check first

1. Confirm the Stripe checkout completed.
2. Refresh after webhook processing.
3. Confirm you are signed into the account that purchased the plan.
4. Contact support if the payment succeeded but credits remain unchanged.
