# Workspace credit problems

Workspace credit display depends on access type.

## Owner sees lower free/shared credits

Check pending or active limited collaborator allocations. Those unused allocations are reserved.

## Collaborator sees fewer credits than owner total

Limited collaborators see their remaining allocation. Full-credit collaborators see free/shared credits, not reserved limited credits.
