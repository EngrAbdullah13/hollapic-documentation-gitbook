# Troubleshooting overview

Start here when Hollapic output or account behavior does not match expectations.

## Common areas

- Generation job failed.
- Product appearance, shape, label, logo, or text changed.
- Reference image was followed too closely.
- Credits or subscription credits did not update.
- OTP email was not received or send limit was reached.
- Workspace invitation, switching, or credit behavior is confusing.
- Billing checkout did not complete.
- Download is unavailable.
