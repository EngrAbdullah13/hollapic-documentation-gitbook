# Product shape changed

Use this guide when bottles, jars, boxes, caps, pouches, or product proportions are altered.

## Fixes

1. Upload a source image where the product shape is fully visible.
2. Avoid heavily cropped or angled source images unless that shape is intentional.
3. Add custom instructions that the shape, proportions, and packaging structure must remain unchanged.
