# Credits were not updated

Credits update from Stripe webhooks, owner credit buckets, and workspace credit calculations.

## Check first

1. Refresh the app.
2. Confirm checkout completed successfully.
3. Make sure you are viewing the correct workspace.
4. If you are a collaborator, remember your visible credits may be limited by access type.
