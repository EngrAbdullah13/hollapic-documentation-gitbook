# OTP email was not received

Use this page when the verification email does not arrive.

## Fixes

1. Check spam or promotions folders.
2. Confirm the email address is typed correctly.
3. Wait for the resend cooldown.
4. Avoid repeated sends that trigger the hourly send limit.
