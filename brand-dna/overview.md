# Brand DNA overview

Brand DNA stores reusable brand context that can guide image generation.

## What Brand DNA stores

The current Brand DNA profile stores:

- Brand or business name.
- What the business sells.
- Customer audience.
- Brand feel selections.
- Preferred colors.
- Website URL.
- A server-built brand summary.
- Completion or skipped state.

## How it works

When Brand DNA is completed and enabled for generation, the backend snapshots the saved brand summary into the generation job and the prompt planner uses it as visual guidance.

## Important behavior

Brand DNA is soft guidance. It must not override product identity, labels, logos, product shape, user instructions, workflow image-order contracts, or safety rules.
