# Brand feel

Brand feel is a multi-select Brand DNA field.

## Current options

- Luxury
- Minimal
- Bold
- Playful
- Natural
- Modern

## How it is used

Selected brand feels are added to the saved brand summary and used as visual guidance during prompt planning.
