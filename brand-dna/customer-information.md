# Customer information

Customer information helps describe the audience for generated visuals.

## Field

`Who are your customers?` is optional.

## How it is used

When provided, the server-built brand summary includes audience context. The prompt planner can use that context to choose visual styling, setting, and tone that fit the target customer.
