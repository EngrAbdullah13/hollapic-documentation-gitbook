# Preferred colors

Preferred colors tell Hollapic which color direction should be treated as brand-friendly.

## Current behavior

Preferred colors are stored as a list and included in the brand summary when provided.

## Best practices

- Use simple color names such as `white`, `sage green`, or `gold`.
- Include only colors that are appropriate for your brand direction.
- Do not use preferred colors to override the actual product packaging color.
