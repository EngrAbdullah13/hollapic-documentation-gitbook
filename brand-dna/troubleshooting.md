# Brand DNA troubleshooting

Use this page when Brand DNA does not appear to affect generation as expected.

## Brand DNA toggle is missing

Confirm that the Brand DNA profile is completed. Skipped or draft profiles do not behave as completed profiles.

## Generated image does not match the brand

Brand DNA is soft guidance. The prompt planner prioritizes workflow rules, uploaded product identity, current user request, and safety rules.

## Website URL is rejected

Use a public `http` or `https` URL. Localhost, private IP addresses, malformed URLs, and non-web protocols are rejected.
