# Custom-credit eligibility

Custom credit purchases are restricted.

## Eligibility rule

A user can buy custom credits only if they have successfully purchased any standard pricing plan at least once in the past or currently have an active/current paid plan.

## Not eligible

The following do not make a user eligible:

- Failed payments.
- Abandoned checkouts.
- Unpaid subscriptions.
- Custom-credit-only purchases.
- Collaborator workspace access.
