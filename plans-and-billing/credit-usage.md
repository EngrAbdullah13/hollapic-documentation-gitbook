# Credit usage

Each generation checks and consumes credits on the backend before the image model is called.

## Deduction order

For owner credit buckets, Hollapic consumes:

1. Subscription credits.
2. Purchased credits.
3. Bonus credits.

## Workspace access

Owner and full-credit collaborators use free/shared credits. Limited collaborators use their own remaining allocation.
