# Billing troubleshooting

Use this page for payment, plan, or credit issues.

## Checkout is blocked

Make sure you are in your own workspace. Collaborators cannot manage billing or buy credits for an owner workspace.

## Custom credit purchase redirects to pricing

You need a successful standard plan purchase history before custom credits are available.

## Credits did not appear

Wait for Stripe webhook processing, then refresh the app. If credits still do not appear, contact support with the account email and approximate checkout time.
