# Purchase custom credits

Custom credits are one-time credit purchases.

## Before you begin

- You must be signed in as the workspace owner.
- You must have successfully purchased a standard pricing plan at least once or currently have a qualifying plan.

## Steps

1. Open Studio.
2. Select the plus credits action.
3. Choose the custom credit amount.
4. Complete Stripe Checkout.

## Expected result

After successful payment, purchased credits are added to the owner’s purchased credit bucket.
