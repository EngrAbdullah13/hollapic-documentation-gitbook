# Credit allocation

Hollapic separates subscription, purchased, and bonus credits.

## Subscription allocation

Successful subscription checkout or renewal sets the subscription credit bucket and expiration timestamp.

## New purchases with team reservations

When an owner receives more credits, those credits become free/shared credits by default. Existing limited collaborator allocations are not automatically increased.
