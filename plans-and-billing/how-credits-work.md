# How credits work

Credits are the unit used for image generation.

## Credit buckets

| Bucket | Behavior |
| --- | --- |
| Subscription credits | Granted by active paid subscriptions and expire/reset by billing period. |
| Purchased credits | One-time custom credit purchases after plan eligibility is met. |
| Bonus credits | Signup credits or internal team credit grants. |

## Visible credits

Visible credits are calculated from the user or active workspace credit context. For owner workspaces, total remaining credits are stored as `creditsRemaining`.
