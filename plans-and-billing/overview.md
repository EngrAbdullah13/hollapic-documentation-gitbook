# Plans overview

Hollapic uses Stripe for plan checkout, custom credit checkout, subscription renewals, and billing webhooks.

## Current plan types

The product includes standard plans plus a User Build Plan where the customer selects a monthly credit amount within the allowed range.

## Owner-only billing

Only the owner of the active workspace can manage billing or buy credits. Collaborators cannot access billing actions for owner workspaces.
