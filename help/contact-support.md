# Contact Support

Contact Hollapic support when the guides do not solve the problem.

## Include the following information

### For image problems

- Your account email
- The workflow you used
- The approximate generation time
- The source and reference images, when safe to share
- A screenshot of the result
- A short description of what should have happened

### For workspace problems

- Your account email
- The workspace name
- Whether you are the owner or collaborator
- A screenshot of the workspace or invitation issue

### For billing problems

- The purchasing email
- The plan or credit purchase
- The approximate payment date and time
- A payment confirmation screenshot
- Do not send full card details

## Protect your information

Never send:

- Passwords
- Email login codes
- Full payment-card numbers
- Secret keys
- Private account credentials
